Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username and email address
  • Password (encrypted with bcrypt)
  • OAuth provider information (if using GitHub/Google login)

Content

  • Scripts you create and upload
  • Public keys you generate or import
  • Script metadata (names, descriptions, visibility settings)

Usage Data

  • Access logs (IP addresses, timestamps)
  • Script retrieval statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To authenticate your account
  • To store and serve your scripts
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage

  • Account data is stored in our database
  • Scripts are stored in S3-compatible storage or local filesystem
  • Private keys are NEVER stored on our servers
  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)

4. Cookies

We use only essential cookies to store your authentication token in your browser's local storage. We do not use tracking or analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

  • OAuth Providers: If you use GitHub or Google login, their privacy policies apply
  • Storage: Scripts may be stored on third-party S3-compatible services

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Export all your data from Account Settings
  • Rectification: Update your account information
  • Erasure: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Download your data in JSON format
  • Object: Contact us to object to data processing

7. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Access logs: Retained for 90 days

8. Security

  • Passwords encrypted with bcrypt
  • JWT tokens for authentication
  • HTTPS encryption for all traffic
  • Private scripts can be encrypted with ChaCha20-Poly1305

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at: privacy@shebang.run
